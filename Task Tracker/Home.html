Task Tracker Web Application

Registration:

    Start by registering an account. Click on the "Create Account" to get Started.
    Fill in the required details: username, password, and email.
    Click the "Save Task" button to register your account.

Saving Tasks:

    After registration, you'll be redirected to a page where you can save tasks.
    Enter a task in the input field and click the "Save Task" button.
    The tasks you save will be associated with your account.

Viewing Tasks:

    To view your saved tasks, click the "View Tasks" button.
